Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Who we are

Seoul Skin Atelier ("we", "us", "our") is a personalized K-beauty skincare curation service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website at seoulskinatelier.com.

2. Information we collect

Before launch, the only personal information we collect is the email address you voluntarily submit through our waitlist form. We may also automatically collect:

  • Basic device and browser information (for site optimization)
  • Aggregated, anonymous usage statistics
  • Referral source and UTM parameters (to understand how you found us)

3. How we use your information

We use your email address solely to:

  • Notify you when Seoul Skin Atelier launches
  • Send you important updates about your waitlist spot
  • Share occasional K-beauty insights and product news (only if you opt in after launch)

We will never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data storage and security

Your email is stored securely in our database powered by Supabase, with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our service, or until you ask us to delete it.

5. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from any of our communications at any time
  • Withdraw your consent

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us at hello@seoulskinatelier.com and we'll respond within 7 days.

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality and basic analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers without your consent.

7. Children's privacy

Seoul Skin Atelier is not directed at children under 14. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. For material changes, we'll notify waitlist subscribers by email.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please email us at hello@seoulskinatelier.com.
